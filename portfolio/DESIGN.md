# Portfolio Visual Direction

## Style Prompt

Premium editor portfolio with a cinematic studio feel: deep navy canvas, crisp blue light, editorial typography, controlled motion, confident spacing, and video-first hierarchy. The experience should feel hand-directed, not template-generated.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#020817`
- Panel: `#061322`
- Elevated panel: `#0a1c31`
- Primary text: `#f5fbff`
- Secondary text: `#c7d7e8`
- Muted text: `#7d92a8`
- Accent blue: `#8edcff`
- Action blue: `#4d9cff`

## Typography

- Primary UI: Inter
- Display: heavy Inter, tight tracking, large editorial scale

## Motion

- Motion exists to guide attention, not decorate.
- Prefer opacity, transform and subtle parallax.
- Avoid cursor gimmicks, confetti, excessive glows and multi-color mood shifts.
- Mobile gets calmer motion and stable spacing.

## What Not To Do

- Do not use red/green/purple product moods in the portfolio UI.
- Do not add random particles or noisy decorative animations.
- Do not make cards look like generic AI glassmorphism.
- Do not change the site structure unless requested.
